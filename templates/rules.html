Recycling Rule Book

Master the art of waste sorting. Follow these guidelines to maximize your eco-points and minimize contamination.

Dry Waste

  • Paper, cardboard, plastics, glass, and metals.
  • Must be clean and dry. Rinse food containers!

Wet Waste

  • Food scraps, fruit peels, tea bags, and flowers.
  • Items should be biodegradable.

E-Waste

  • Dead batteries, old chargers, broken phones.
  • Never throw in regular bins. Use E-waste hubs.

Hazardous

  • Paint cans, chemicals, pesticides, oils.
  • Toxic waste requires special disposal handle.

Medical

  • Masks, gloves, expired pills, old bandages.
  • Biohazard materials must be securely bagged.

Uncertain?

  • Use our AI Scanner to be 100% sure.
  • Check local municipal apps for hyper-local rules.
